All About Grass

Discover the fascinating world of grasses that cover our planet

What is Grass?

Grasses are monocotyledonous plants that belong to the family Poaceae. They are one of the most important plant groups on Earth, covering about 20% of the planet's land surface.

Types of Grass

Lawn Grass

Perfect for landscaping and creating beautiful green spaces around homes and parks.

Ornamental Grass

Decorative varieties that add texture and movement to gardens and landscapes.

Native Grass

Indigenous species that support local ecosystems and wildlife.

Amazing Grass Facts

  • Grass can live for hundreds of years
  • There are over 10,000 species of grass worldwide
  • Grass produces more oxygen than trees per square foot
  • Bamboo is actually a type of grass
  • Grass can grow on every continent except Antarctica

Grass Care Tips

Keep your grass healthy and vibrant with these essential tips:

Watering

Water deeply but infrequently to encourage deep root growth.

Mowing

Never cut more than one-third of the grass blade height at once.

Fertilizing

Apply fertilizer in early spring and fall for best results.
