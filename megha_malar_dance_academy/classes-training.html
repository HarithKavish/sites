Classes

Training is offered in a structured and progressive format for all age groups and levels.

Kids / Beginners (Age 5+)

From introduction to Bharatanatyam through rhythm, movement, and foundational discipline in a nurturing environment to systematic training focusing on technique, coordination, and cultural understanding.

Adults / Advanced

Systematic training for adults new to Bharatanatyam and rigorous training in technique, abhinaya, and performance repertoire for committed students.

Student Training Journey

Students receive structured training in:

  • Adavus (foundational technique)
  • Abhinaya (expressional storytelling)
  • Nritta (pure dance technique)
  • Nrittya (expressive interpretation)
  • Classical Repertoire (performance compositions)

The learning journey progresses from foundational technique to expressive storytelling, culminating in refined stage performance and artistic maturity.
