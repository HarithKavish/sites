About the Academy

Aitva Nritya Academy (ANA) is dedicated to preserving and carrying forward the rich tradition of Bharatanatyam through disciplined training, artistic expression, and the Guru-Shishya parampara.

Founded by Vidushi Megha Malar Prabhakar, the academy is rooted in the Kalakshetra tradition and offers a structured, immersive learning experience that balances technical precision with expressive depth.

With a strong emphasis on heritage, authenticity, and cultural continuity, the academy nurtures the next generation of dancers-guiding students to not only learn the art form, but to embody its values, discipline, and timeless artistic spirit.
