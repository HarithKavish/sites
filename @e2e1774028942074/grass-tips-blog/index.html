Spring Lawn Care Essentials

Spring is the perfect time to prepare your lawn for the growing season. Start by removing debris, aerating compacted soil, and applying a balanced fertilizer.

Don't forget to sharpen your mower blades and set the cutting height to 2.5-3 inches for most grass types.

Published: March 15, 2024

Watering Wisdom

Deep, infrequent watering encourages strong root growth. Water early in the morning to reduce evaporation and prevent disease.

Most lawns need about 1 inch of water per week, including rainfall.

Published: March 10, 2024

Quick Grass Tips

  • Mow high - longer grass shades weeds and develops deeper roots
  • Leave clippings on the lawn - they return nutrients to the soil
  • Aerate compacted soil annually
  • Choose the right grass type for your climate
  • Test your soil every 2-3 years
